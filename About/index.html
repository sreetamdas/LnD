English Literary and Debating Club NITW

Literary and Debating Club (LDC) aspires to be a platform where every individual is nurtured to realize his potential, where there is an atmosphere of joyous learning, responsibility to society, dedication to hard work, commitment to be tolerant, compassionate and inclusive.

Journey of LDC
